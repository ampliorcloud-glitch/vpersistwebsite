# 9. Design Style Options (Mockups)

## Website Design Directions

Below are four proposed design directions for the vPersist website. Review each style and select one (or a combination) as the basis for final design development.

---

### Style 1: Clean Minimal Corporate

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Feel** | Professional & Approachable |
| **Best For** | Traditional B2B buyers |

White background, electric blue accents, dual CTA buttons side by side, feature cards below hero. Professional and approachable — ideal for traditional B2B buyers.

---

### Style 2: Dark Bold Premium

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Feel** | Premium & Tech-forward |
| **Best For** | Tech-savvy, high-ticket clients |

Dark navy background throughout, glowing data/network graphic, orange accent highlights and CTA buttons. Below the hero: light grey service cards.

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|

| Attribute | Detail |

50/50 split hero — white left panel with headline + CTA, solid blue right panel with feature icons. Full-width orange RTaaS banner below, then cards section.

# 12. Colour Scheme (Bright, Premium, Structured)

## Colour Palette

*Bright website + clarity — premium, structured, readable.*

---

### Primary Colors

| Swatch | Colour Name | Hex Code | Meaning |
|--------|-------------|----------|---------|
| 🔵 | Deep Blue | `#1F3A8A` | Reliability · System · Control |
| 🟢 | Bright Teal / Aqua | `#14B8A6` | Execution · Freshness · Modern |

---

### Accent Color (Highlight / CTA)

| Swatch | Colour Name | Hex Code | Use For |
|--------|-------------|----------|---------|
| 🟡 | Warm Yellow / Amber | `#F59E0B` | CTA buttons · Highlights · Key sections |

---

### Background Colors

| Swatch | Colour Name | Hex Code | Use For |
|--------|-------------|----------|---------|
| ⚪ | White | `#FFFFFF` | Primary page background |
| 🔲 | Light Grey | `#F3F4F6` | Alternate section backgrounds |

---

### Text Colors

| Colour Name | Hex Code | Use For |
|-------------|----------|---------|
| Primary Text | `#111827` | Main body text — almost black |
| Secondary Text | `#6B7280` | Subtext, captions, labels |

---

### Color Strategy

> **Recommended Ratio**
>
> - **70%** White — clean, bright, premium, readable
> - **20%** Deep Blue — trust, structure, control
> - **10%** Accent (Teal + Amber) — energy, CTA, highlights
>
> 👉 This keeps the site: clean | bright | premium | readable

# 13. Typography (Very Important for Feel)

## Headings

**Font:** Inter / Poppins

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Style | Modern, clean, tech + business mix |
| Weight | Bold for headlines |
| Spacing | Short lines, plenty of spacing |

---

## Body Text

**Font:** Inter / Open Sans

| ✅ Do | ❌ Avoid |
|-------|---------|
| Short lines | Dense paragraphs |
| Plenty of spacing | Long unbroken blocks |
| Clean and readable | Small font sizes on mobile |

# 14. Visual Language (How Website Should Look)

## A. Layout Style

👉 **Clean + Structured (NOT cluttered)**

- Large white space
- Clear sections
- Sharp blocks

---

## B. Use "System" Visuals — Not Stock Photos

Instead of stock photos, use:

- Flow diagrams
- Layered blocks
- Process visuals

> **Example Process Visual**
>
> ```
> Talent
>    ↓
> QC / Supervision
>    ↓
> Data + Tools
>    ↓
> Revenue Output
> ```
>
> 👉 This communicates your model instantly

---

## C. Icons Style

- Simple line icons
- Consistent style
- Minimal color

---

## D. Avoid

- ❌ Handshake stock images
- ❌ Random office photos
- ❌ Generic HR visuals
